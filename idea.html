I first came up with the idea of solar powered phones while talking with some friends. They said that their chargers often didn't work or that their phones died in places charging wasn't possible or convenient. Then, later that week, the power went out while my phone was on low battery. This told me that I needed to make a change. The solar powered phone is able to not only charge but save extra power for cloudy days or night time. We also sell solar powered chargers to use with the phones.
